My Bio

Born as a November baby and raised in Rhode Island, I was the second child as a citizen of the United State of my family's first generation. My mother and father moved to the United States from overseas of Southeast Asia to live a better life for their children and their relatives. Due to the fact that my parents spoke a language other than English, I became a bilingual speaker of Hmong and English.

I grew up living in Providence, the town of Rhode Island. Living with my family, I developed a passion of art and gaming. With art I became serious about my passion and won a scholarship in 2018 to attend a RISD Teen Class, funded by the Rhode Island Foundation. With gaming, I've developed an inspiration and strong interest of game development from Undertale in middle school. I've decided to pursue a gaming major at New England Institute of Technology.
